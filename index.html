The Day

A holy woman, remembered by holy women.

Saint Veronica is remembered for a single act of quiet courage — stepping through a hostile crowd to wipe the face of Christ on the road to Calvary. That small gesture of tenderness is the heart of our guild.

This past weekend, our sisters gathered in her name — in song, in prayer, in white and sky-blue, and in the simple joy of being together. This page is our little way of bringing the day home to those who couldn't be there.

A Word of Welcome

From the President of the Guild

Welcome to our St Veronica Day celebration page. Today we give thanks for faith, fellowship, service and the beautiful bond shared by the women of our guild.

May these memories remind every sister that her prayers, kindness and presence are a blessing to the church and to the community we serve.

President, St Veronica Women's Guild
